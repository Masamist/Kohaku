Authentic Japanese Catering, Tailored to Your Occasion

We bring the flavours of Japan to your event with beautifully crafted dishes, prepared fresh on-site by Chef Kosuke. From corporate functions to private celebrations, each menu is tailored with traditional techniques, seasonal ingredients, and a personal touch to make your event truly special.

About Our Chef

Meet Chef Kosuke
Passion on Every Plate

Originally from Kyoto, Japan, Chef Kosuke has over 20 years of experience—10 in Japan and 10 in New Zealand. Starting his culinary journey at 16, he’s mastered everything from elegant weddings to private functions. Chef Kosuke brings his passion for quality ingredients and knowledge of the craft to every dish he prepares, creating a delicious and unique dining experience. Friendly, professional, and flexible, he works closely with clients to craft menus that suit any taste or occasion.

About Our Story

Japanese Crafted with Care

At the heart of our catering is a deep respect for tradition, ingredients, and the people we serve. Every dish is handcrafted from scratch, using carefully selected ingredients that reflect the seasons and the essence of Japanese cuisine. We take the time to prepare each menu with care and intention—designed specifically for your occasion and your guests. Discover the difference that true craftsmanship and hospitality can make.

Tailored to Every Guest

We want every guest to enjoy an unforgettable experience. That’s why we personalise each menu—whether it’s adding a fusion twist or adjusting for allergies and dietary needs. From vegetarian sushi to custom alternatives, 'we’re happy to create dishes everyone can enjoy.

Artwork on Your Plate

We believe a meal should spark joy. Chef Kosuke treats each dish as a form of art—crafted with precision, creativity, and heart. From beautiful presentation to thoughtful flavours, every plate is designed to surprise, delight, and make your guests smile.
